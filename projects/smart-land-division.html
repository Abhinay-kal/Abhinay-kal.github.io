Overview

Smart Land Division applies spatial constraints to propose practical parcel split options. The project focuses on predictable geometry decisions instead of ad-hoc manual partitioning.

Impact

  • Applied repeatable geometry logic for better decision consistency.
  • Created clearer outputs for map-based planning workflows.
